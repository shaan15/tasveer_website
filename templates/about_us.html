About Tasveer

Tasveer - The photography society of IIIT-Delhi aims to gather all those people who genuinely love photography. It is a platform for students and faculty to discuss photography and its related aspects. We conduct Intra as well as inter Institute Competitions, workshops as well as photowalks. We are also regularly joined by many alumni and it is a good learning experience to interact with them - not just limited to photography. We also go on a lot of trips and as already mentioned, photowalks - which is a welcome relief to the hectic college life. Most importantly, we have a lot of fun.

This past year has been an active one. Tasveer has organized numerous photowalks, covered almost all institute events, organized two photo-trips and also organized one workshop in the institute.
